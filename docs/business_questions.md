# Business Questions

## Executive Questions

- How much revenue are we generating by day and month?
- How many orders are completed, cancelled, and returned?
- What is the average order value?
- Which regions generate the most sales?

## Product Questions

- Which products sell the most units?
- Which products generate the most net revenue?
- Which categories are growing or underperforming?
- Which brands contribute the most revenue?

## Customer Questions

- Who are the top customers by lifetime value?
- What percentage of customers place repeat orders?
- How long has it been since each customer last purchased?
- Which countries and cities have the highest-value customers?

## Payment Questions

- What is the payment success rate?
- Which payment methods are most common?
- Are failed payments concentrated in specific regions or methods?

## Operations Questions

- What is the cancellation rate?
- What is the return rate?
- Which regions have the highest order volume?
